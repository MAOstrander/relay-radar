Why should I log in?

While you can still see all currently posted Relays without logging in, signing up and loggin in offers the ability to post new Relays you find and coordinate with others for the optimum 3DS gaming experience!

What are the benefits of loggin in? Ability to add relay locations.

Ability to save your favorite relay locations.

ability to checkin and let people know you are at a particular relay.

an individual profile with optional information you can share.

Ability to find and message other members for gaming and coordination reasons.
